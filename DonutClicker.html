Donut Clicker!

Welcome to the donut clicker! In this game you click the "Bake a donut"
button to bake a donut and sell it for money! You can buy Bakers to
slowly help you bake more donuts, or buy a new stove so YOU can make
more donuts! Become the biggest donut shop in the nation!Can you earn 1
million dollars? Well, what are you waiting for? START BAKING!!!
